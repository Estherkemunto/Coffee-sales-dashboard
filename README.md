# Coffee-sales-dashboard
Coffee sales data analysis and dashboard using Google Sheets

#Geographic Performance

The United States dominates total revenue, significantly outperforming Ireland and the United Kingdom. This indicates that the business is highly dependent on the U.S. market for sales.

#Coffee Type Performance

Coffee types Exc and Lib generate the highest sales, closely followed by Ara, while Rob records the lowest performance. This suggests stronger customer preference for premium or specific blends.

#Roast Type Trends

Light roast coffee generates the highest revenue, outperforming both medium and dark roasts. This indicates a clear customer preference for lighter roast profiles.

#Sales Trend Over Time

Sales show fluctuating but recurring peaks over time, suggesting seasonal or periodic demand patterns rather than steady growth.

#Customer Contribution

A small group of customers, led by Allis Wilmore, contributes the highest share of revenue, indicating that top customers play a key role in overall sales performance.

#Overall Sales Performance

The business generated $45,134 in total revenue from 1,000 orders, with 3,551 units sold, reflecting consistent transaction activity and steady product demand.
